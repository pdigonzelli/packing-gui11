
**** Installation of WS-Pluspack *****

1. Copy/unzip the plus-directory with subdirectories to somewhere on the propath.
2a. Go to Webspeed Workshop and run from Scripting-Lab "run plus/setup.p."
2b. .. or .. just change to plus/setup.p in the URL.


If you wish to use the startup enhancer then look for
information in the Pluspack's help pages.
